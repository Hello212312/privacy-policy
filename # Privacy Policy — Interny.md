# Privacy Policy — Interny
Last updated May 13, 2026

The short version: we collect very little data. Most of what you enter stays on your device and never leaves it. We do not sell your data, we do not show you ads, and we do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 1. Overview & Our Commitment

Interny is committed to protecting the privacy of all users, particularly minors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the App and should be read alongside our Terms of Service.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide a personalized experience.

Information you provide directly during onboarding or profile setup:
- Your first name (used to personalize in-app greetings)
- Your current grade (9th–12th) to filter eligible programs
- Your areas of interest (e.g., STEM, Business, Art) to power recommendations
- Your city, state, or "remote only" preference to surface relevant listings

Information generated through your use of the App, stored locally on your device:
- Internships you have saved or marked as top picks
- Application status labels you assign (e.g., Applied, Interviewing, Accepted)
- Personal notes you write about any listing
- Internship listings you have viewed

We do not collect your email address, phone number, password, date of birth, Social Security number, financial information, or any government-issued identification.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used exclusively to deliver and improve the App experience. Specifically, we use it to:

- Personalize your home feed and search results based on your grade, location, and interests
- Calculate match scores between your profile and available listings
- Display your saved items, application pipeline, and upcoming deadlines
- Send push notifications for deadline reminders, if you have enabled this feature
- Improve the relevance and quality of listings over time using aggregated, non-identifiable usage patterns

We do not use your information for advertising, behavioral tracking, or any commercial purpose other than operating the App as described.

---

## 4. How We Store Your Information

All profile data, saved listings, notes, and application statuses are stored locally on your device using the device's built-in secure storage. This data does not transit our servers and is not accessible to us.

This means:
- Your data is private to your device — no one else, including Interny, can access it
- Uninstalling the App, performing a factory reset, or using "Sign Out" will permanently and irreversibly delete all your data
- We cannot restore lost data, as we hold no copies
- Your data does not sync across multiple devices

We do not operate a user database or cloud storage for personal profile data. Your information lives on your phone, under your control.

---

## 5. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what we do not collect:

- We do not collect your email address or create user accounts
- We do not track your precise GPS location — location is a city/state field you type manually
- We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IP addresses for tracking
- We do not use cookies or cross-app tracking technologies
- We do not collect the content of your personal notes
- We do not collect payment information of any kind — the App is entirely free
- We do not collect biometric data, photos, microphone input, or contact information
