## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Do I need an account to use Interny?**
No. Interny does not require an account or email address. Just open the app, complete the short onboarding, and you're ready to go.

**What information do I need to provide?**
During onboarding you'll be asked for your first name, current grade (9th–12th), areas of interest, and your state. All of this is stored locally on your device and used only to personalize your results.

---

### Internship Listings

**Where do the internships come from?**
Listings are curated and sourced from real third-party organizations. We update them regularly, but you should always verify deadlines and eligibility directly with the organization before applying.

**Why isn't a specific program showing up?**
Check your active filters — a field, pay type, or location filter may be excluding it. Tap the filter icon on the Search screen and clear any active filters to see all listings.

**A deadline or detail looks wrong. What should I do?**
Email us at blah78981@gmail.com with the program name and we'll verify and update it as quickly as possible.

---

### Saving & Tracking

**How do I save an internship?**
Tap the bookmark icon on any listing card or on the detail screen. Saved internships appear in the Tracker tab.

**How do I update my application status?**
Go to the Tracker tab, find the internship, and tap to update its status (Saved → Applying → Submitted → Interviewing → Accepted / Rejected / Waitlisted).

**Can I add notes to a listing?**
Yes. Open any listing's detail screen and scroll to the notes section. Notes are saved locally on your device only.

---

### Deadlines & Notifications

**How do I add a deadline to my calendar?**
On any listing's detail screen, tap "Add to Calendar." The app will ask for calendar permission the first time. This creates a single event on your device's calendar.

**How do push notifications work?**
If you enable notifications, Interny sends you reminders at 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before the deadline for each internship you've saved. You can turn this off at any time in the Profile screen under Settings.

**I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?**
1. Open your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Interny and make sure notifications are allowed.
2. In the Interny app, go to Profile → Settings and confirm notifications are toggled on.
3. Make sure the internship is saved — only saved listings trigger reminders.

---

### Data & Privacy

**Where is my data stored?**
Everything — your profile, saved listings, notes, and application statuses — is stored locally on your device. It never leaves your phone and is not stored on any Interny server.

**How do I delete all my data?**
Go to Profile → Settings → Sign Out. This permanently and irreversibly deletes all locally stored data including your profile, saved listings, notes, and application history.

**Can I recover my data after signing out or uninstalling?**
No. Because data is stored only on your device, we have no copies and cannot restore it.

---

### Technical Issues

**The app is showing no internships / a blank screen.**
This usually means the app couldn't connect to the internet on startup. Check your connection and pull down to refresh on the Home screen. If the problem persists, close and reopen the app.

**The app is crashing or behaving unexpectedly.**
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, email us at blah78981@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version and we'll look into it.
